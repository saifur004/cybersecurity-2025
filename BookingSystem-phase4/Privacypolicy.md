# Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the web-based Booking System processes personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). The system follows the principles of **Privacy by Design** and **Privacy by Default**.

This policy applies to all users of the Booking System, including reservers and administrators.

---

## 2. Data Controller

The system provider is responsible for the processing of personal data within this Booking System and acts as the **data controller** under GDPR.

---

## 3. Personal Data Collected

The Booking System collects and processes the following personal data during registration and usage:

- **Username** (email address)
- **Password** (stored as a secure hash)
- **Date of birth** (used only for age verification)
- **User role** (reserver or administrator)
- **Acceptance of terms of service**
- **Account creation timestamp**
- **Session identifiers** (UUID tokens)

**No additional personal data** such as address, phone number, or identification numbers are collected.

---

## 4. Purpose of Processing

Personal data is processed for the following purposes:

- User authentication and account management
- Verifying that users are over 15 years old before allowing reservations
- Managing resource reservations
- Enforcing role-based access control
- Maintaining system security and audit logs

**Personal data is not processed for marketing or profiling purposes.**

---

## 5. Legal Basis for Processing

The legal bases for processing personal data are:

- **User consent**, provided during registration by accepting the Terms of Service
- **Contractual necessity** for providing the booking service
- **Legal obligation** to restrict access for users under 15 years of age
- **Legitimate interest** in securing and maintaining the system

---

## 6. Booking Visibility and Data Sharing

- Booked resources are visible to non-logged-in users **without displaying any personal data**
- Logged-in users may see the email address of the reserver associated with a booking
- **Personal data is not shared with third parties**

---

## 7. Data Retention

- User data is stored as long as the user account exists
- When a user account is deleted by an administrator, all related reservations are automatically removed from the system
- Log data is retained for security and audit purposes

---

## 8. Data Security

The system implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, including:

- Secure password hashing
- Role-based access control
- Database constraints and triggers to enforce age restrictions
- Use of pseudonymous identifiers (UUIDs) in reservations
- Secure session handling

---

## 9. User Rights Under GDPR

Users have the right to:

- **Access** their personal data
- **Request correction** of inaccurate personal data
- **Request deletion** of their personal data via an administrator
- **Be informed** about how their personal data is processed

Requests related to personal data can be made by contacting the system administrator.

---

## 10. Cookies

The Booking System uses **essential cookies** required for authentication and session management. No tracking or marketing cookies are used.

Further information is available in the **Cookie Policy**.

---

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in the system or legal requirements. Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically.

---

## 12. Contact Information

For questions related to privacy and data protection, users should contact the **system administrator**.
