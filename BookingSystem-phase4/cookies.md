# Cookie Policy

## 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how cookies are used in the web-based Booking System and how they relate to user privacy and data protection under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

---

## 2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on a user's device when visiting a website. They are commonly used to ensure proper functionality, security, and user authentication.

---

## 3. Cookies Used by the Booking System

The Booking System uses **only essential cookies** required for the system to function correctly.

The following types of cookies are used:

### Session Cookies
Used to maintain user login sessions and authentication state.

### Security Cookies
Used to protect the system against unauthorized access and ensure secure communication.

**No advertising, analytics, or tracking cookies are used.**

---

## 4. Purpose of Cookies

Cookies are used solely to:

- Authenticate users after login
- Maintain active user sessions
- Protect the system against security threats

**Cookies are not used for profiling or marketing purposes.**

---

## 5. Legal Basis for Cookie Usage

Since the system uses only **strictly necessary cookies**, explicit user consent is not required under GDPR. The legal basis for processing is **legitimate interest** in providing a secure and functional service.

---

## 6. Third-Party Cookies

The Booking System **does not use third-party cookies** or external tracking services.

---

## 7. Managing Cookies

Users can control or delete cookies through their browser settings. **Disabling cookies may affect the functionality** of the Booking System, including login and reservation features.

---

## 8. Changes to This Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated if cookie usage changes. Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically.

---

## 9. Contact Information

For questions related to cookie usage and privacy, users should contact the **system administrator**.
